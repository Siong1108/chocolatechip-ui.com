ChocolateChip-UI helps you create the Web app you want. It uses a straightforward markup language called WAML that makes it crystal clear what you are building. This markup also tells ChocolateChip-UI what functionality to add. ChUI.js and ChUI.css add all the magic in the background with minimal input from you.

While developing your app with ChocolateChip-UI you can test it on your desktop with mouse interaction using Google Chrome, Apple Safari or any other Webkit-based desktop browser. Of course you can also test it in appropriate device emulators as well on actual devices to see the impact orientation change will have on your app.
